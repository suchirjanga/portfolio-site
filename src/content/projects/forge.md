Forge is a habit, recovery, and performance tracking platform. Built with
Next.js, React, TypeScript, Prisma, and Auth.js. It connects with fitness
devices and health data to surface sleep, recovery, and training load right
next to daily habits.

A pure scoring engine turns that signal into a clear read on consistency and
readiness over time, so building discipline and optimizing training become a
single loop — log the work, read the recovery, adjust. Shipped and live.
