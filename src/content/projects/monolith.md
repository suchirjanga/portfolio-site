Monolith is a cinematic 3D fitness experience. Built with Next.js, React Three
Fiber, GSAP, and Lenis. Strength, discipline, and progression are told through
a scroll-driven sequence where the camera moves through a dark, monolithic
space and form resolves out of shadow.

The visual language is brutalist and minimal — heavy typography, a single amber
light, and weight you can feel. Nothing moves unless the scroll earns it.
Shipped and live.
