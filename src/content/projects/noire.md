Noire is a luxury 3D watch experience. Built with Next.js and React Three
Fiber, with a procedurally modelled timepiece. Craftsmanship, precision, and
timeless design are revealed through cinematic product storytelling as the
watch turns slowly through light.

The palette is black and gold, the lighting cinematic, the pacing patient —
a campaign film for an object that is all about time. Shipped and live.
