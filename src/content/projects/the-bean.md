A single-page site for a coffee brand. Built with Vite, React, and
TypeScript, using motion/react together with GSAP ScrollTrigger. Set in
Fraunces and Inter.

The centrepiece is a scroll-pinned, five-panel horizontal sequence that
walks through the brewing journey — bean, grind, bloom, pour, cup. All
of the photography on the site was personally collected and shot by me.
Shipped and live.
